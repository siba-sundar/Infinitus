About

The 2025 edition of Infinitus promises to be a defining moment in the festival’s illustrious journey, bringing together a vibrant community of over 12,000 students from diverse backgrounds. With an expected participation of more than 7,000 individuals in an array of competitions and showcases, this year’s event is set to ignite unparalleled creativity and innovation. The digital reach of Infinitus '25 aims to surpass all milestones, targeting over 20,000 engagements across social media platforms, as attendees share their exhilarating moments and experiences. The official website is primed to serve as a central hub for updates, with projections of attracting more than 8,000 visitors, reflecting the growing excitement and anticipation surrounding the event. Infinitus '25 is not just a festival; it’s a cultural phenomenon, building on its legacy to celebrate diversity, talent, and the unifying spirit of festivity, setting an even higher benchmark for the years to come.
